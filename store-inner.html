Connect your Shopify store to Printful

Step 1: Create your Shopify account Step 2: Install the Printful app on Shopify Step 3: Connect your store Step 4: Add your products

Follow these steps to connect your Shopify store to your Printful account:

Step 1: Create your Shopify account

Head over to Shopify.com and create an account if you don’t have one yet.

Disclaimer: Printful might earn a commission from signups or purchases completed via this link. Read more here

Step 2: Install the Printful app on Shopify

1. Click the Get the Printful app link below:

Get the app

2. Log in to your Shopify account.

3. Click on the Printful app.

4. Next, click Add app and confirm the installation.

Click Install app and you’re ready for the next part!

Step 3: Connect your store

After you install the app, you’ll be asked to log into both your Shopify and Printful accounts. Just follow the steps and connect the two together.

Step 4: Add your products

To start accepting orders you need to publish your products to Shopify. On Printful, head over to Dashboard > Stores and select your Shopify store. Click Add product and choose the products you want to sell on Shopify.

That's it. You're done!

Your new Shopify orders will now be automatically imported into Printful!

Note that we automatically configure your shipping settings for your Shopify store, but if you have any different requirements to the predetermined ones you can always change them manually.
